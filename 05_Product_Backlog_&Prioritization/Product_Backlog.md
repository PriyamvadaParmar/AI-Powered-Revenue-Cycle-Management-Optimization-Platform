# Product Backlog  
## AI-Powered Revenue Cycle Management (RCM) Product

---

## 1. Purpose

This document represents the **active and planned product backlog** for the AI-Powered RCM Product.

The backlog translates product epics into **prioritized, actionable work items** and serves as the primary input for sprint planning and delivery execution.

---

## 2. Backlog Structure

Backlog items are organized by:
- Epic alignment  
- Priority (Now / Next / Later)  
- Business outcome  

Each item will be further decomposed into user stories and tasks during sprint grooming.

## 6. Backlog Governance

- Backlog is owned by Product Management  
- Items are refined during grooming sessions  
- Priority is adjusted based on business needs and performance metrics  
- Delivery capacity and dependencies are considered during sprint planning  

---

## 7. Definition of Ready (DoR)

A backlog item is considered ready when:
- Business value is clearly defined  
- Acceptance criteria are documented  
- Dependencies are identified  
- Effort estimation is available  

---

## 8. Summary

This backlog ensures continuous delivery of **high-impact capabilities** while maintaining alignment with business objectives and operational realities.
