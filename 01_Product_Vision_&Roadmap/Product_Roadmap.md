# Product Roadmap  
## AI-Powered Revenue Cycle Management (RCM) Product

---

## 1. Purpose of This Roadmap

This document outlines the **product roadmap** for the AI-Powered RCM platform, providing a forward-looking view of product evolution aligned with business goals, operational needs, and customer impact.

The roadmap is used to:
- Align stakeholders on product direction
- Guide prioritization and release planning
- Balance short-term operational improvements with long-term strategic capabilities

---

## 2. Product Vision (Recap)

> To enable healthcare providers to proactively optimize their revenue cycle through AI-driven insights, automated workflows, and real-time visibility—reducing revenue leakage and accelerating cash flow at scale.

---

## 3. Roadmap Principles

The roadmap is guided by the following principles:

- **Business Impact First:** Prioritize features with measurable revenue or efficiency impact  
- **Proactive Over Reactive:** Shift from denial management to denial prevention  
- **Automation with Control:** Automate routine work while preserving human oversight  
- **Incremental Value Delivery:** Deliver value in small, frequent releases  
- **Scalability & Compliance:** Ensure enterprise-grade performance, security, and HIPAA compliance  

---

## 4. Roadmap Overview (High-Level)

| Timeframe | Focus Area |
|---------|-----------|
| Now | Stabilization & Core Optimization |
| Near-Term | Intelligence & Automation Expansion |
| Mid-Term | Advanced Analytics & Predictive Insights |
| Long-Term | Platform Intelligence & Ecosystem Integration |

---

## 5. Detailed Roadmap

### Current Release (Now)

**Theme:** Core RCM Optimization & Platform Stability

**Key Deliverables:**
- Denial risk scoring for pre-submission claims  
- Smart AR worklists with AI-based prioritization  
- End-to-end RCM operational dashboard  
- Workflow automation for common denial categories  
- SLA monitoring and alerts  
- Performance and scalability improvements  

**Primary Outcomes:**
- Reduced denial rates  
- Improved AR efficiency  
- Increased platform adoption by ops teams  

---

###  Near-Term (Next 1–2 Quarters)

**Theme:** Intelligence Expansion & Operational Automation

**Planned Capabilities:**
- Enhanced denial reason classification using ML models  
- Payer-specific rules engine configuration  
- Auto-suggested corrective actions for high-risk claims  
- Configurable dashboards by role (Ops, AR, Leadership)  
- Improved audit logs and traceability  

**Expected Impact:**
- Higher First Pass Yield  
- Reduced manual intervention  
- Faster issue resolution  

---

### Mid-Term (3–6 Quarters)

**Theme:** Advanced Predictive Analytics & Decision Support

**Planned Capabilities:**
- Predictive cash flow forecasting  
- Trend analysis by payer, provider, and specialty  
- Root cause analysis for recurring denials  
- Capacity planning insights for ops leadership  
- Cross-client benchmarking (anonymized)  

**Expected Impact:**
- Strategic revenue optimization  
- Data-driven workforce planning  
- Improved executive decision-making  

---

###  Long-Term (6–12 Quarters)

**Theme:** Intelligent RCM Platform & Ecosystem Integration

**Planned Capabilities:**
- Self-learning AI models with continuous feedback loops  
- Intelligent workflow orchestration across RCM stages  
- Plug-and-play integrations with EHR and payer platforms  
- Predictive SLA breach prevention  
- Platform extensibility via APIs  

**Expected Impact:**
- Autonomous RCM operations  
- Strong competitive differentiation  
- Long-term scalability and innovation  

---

## 6. Dependencies & Risks

### Key Dependencies
- Availability and quality of historical claims data  
- Adoption of AI-driven workflows by operations teams  
- Engineering capacity and data science maturity  

### Key Risks
- Model accuracy impacting trust and adoption  
- Change management challenges in ops teams  
- Regulatory or payer rule changes  

Mitigation plans are tracked in delivery and sprint artifacts.

---

## 7. Roadmap Governance

- Roadmap reviewed quarterly with leadership  
- Adjusted based on performance metrics and client feedback  
- Features move into delivery only after value validation  
- Roadmap changes are communicated cross-functionally
