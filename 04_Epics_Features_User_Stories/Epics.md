# Product Epics  
## AI-Powered Revenue Cycle Management (RCM) Product

---

## 1. Purpose

This document defines the **major product epics** for the AI-Powered RCM Product.

Epics represent **large, outcome-driven bodies of work** that deliver measurable business value and are broken down into features and user stories for execution.

---

## 2. Epic Structuring Principles

- Each epic aligns to a clear business outcome  
- Epics are scoped to be deliverable across multiple sprints  
- Epics are prioritized based on revenue impact, risk reduction, and operational efficiency  
- Epics evolve as product usage and feedback increase  

---

## 3. Active Epics (Now)

### EPIC-01: Denial Risk Prediction & Prevention

**Business Objective:**  
Reduce preventable claim denials by identifying high-risk claims before submission.

**Scope Includes:**
- Denial risk scoring models  
- Predicted denial reason classification  
- Risk threshold configuration  
- Pre-submission review workflows  

**Key Outcomes:**
- Improved First Pass Yield  
- Reduced rework and manual corrections  

---

### EPIC-02: Intelligent AR Worklist Prioritization

**Business Objective:**  
Reduce Accounts Receivable (A/R) days by focusing effort on the highest-impact claims.

**Scope Includes:**
- AI-ranked AR queues  
- SLA-based alerts and escalation  
- Value- and risk-based prioritization logic  

**Key Outcomes:**
- Faster cash realization  
- Improved AR team productivity  

---

### EPIC-03: Unified RCM Visibility & Dashboards

**Business Objective:**  
Provide real-time, end-to-end visibility into RCM performance for operations and leadership.

**Scope Includes:**
- Operational dashboards for RCM teams  
- Executive dashboards for leadership  
- Drill-down reporting by payer, provider, and specialty  

**Key Outcomes:**
- Data-driven decision-making  
- Early identification of bottlenecks  

---

### EPIC-04: Workflow Automation & Exception Management

**Business Objective:**  
Reduce manual effort and standardize RCM workflows while preserving human oversight.

**Scope Includes:**
- Automated claim and denial routing  
- Exception-based workflows  
- Configurable business rules  

**Key Outcomes:**
- Reduced operational cost  
- Consistent execution across teams  

---

## 6. Epic Dependencies & Considerations

- Availability of historical claims data  
- Accuracy and explainability of AI models

---

## 7. Next Steps

Each epic will be decomposed into:
- Features  
- User Stories  
- Acceptance Criteria  

These will be tracked and prioritized in the **Product Backlog & Prioritization** artifacts.
