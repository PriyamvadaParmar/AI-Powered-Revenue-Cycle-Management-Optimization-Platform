## 3. Delivered Features

### EPIC-01: Denial Risk Prediction & Prevention
- Denial risk score displayed at claim level  
- Predicted denial reason surfaced for high-risk claims  
- Risk-based filtering enabled for operational review  

**Impact:**  
Enabled proactive identification of high-risk claims prior to submission.

---

### EPIC-02: Intelligent AR Worklist Prioritization
- AI-ranked AR worklists generated based on value, risk, and aging  
- Claim value and aging indicators displayed  

**Impact:**  
Improved focus on high-impact AR items and reduced manual prioritization effort.

---

### EPIC-03: Unified RCM Visibility & Dashboards
- Initial KPI dashboard delivered:
  - Claim Denial Rate  
  - Accounts Receivable (A/R) Aging  

**Impact:**  
Provided early operational visibility into RCM performance.
